Our Ministries

Serving God by serving others

Prayer Ministry

Our prayer ministry is dedicated to interceding for the church, community, and nations. Join us as we seek God's face and believe for miracles.

  • Early Morning Prayer: 5:30 AM Daily
  • Friday Night Vigil: 10:00 PM
  • Prayer Chain Network

Worship Ministry

Leading the congregation in heartfelt worship through music, dance, and creative arts. Our worship team meets weekly for practice and preparation.

  • Practice: Thursdays 4:00 PM
  • Audio/Visual Team
  • Dance and Drama

Children's Ministry

Nurturing the next generation in the ways of the Lord through age-appropriate teaching, activities, and discipleship.

  • Sunday School: During main services
  • Vacation Bible School
  • Children's Camp

Youth Ministry

Engaging teenagers and young adults with relevant biblical teaching and creating a community where they can grow in their faith.

  • Youth Service: Fridays 6:00 PM
  • Bible Study: Tuesdays 4:00 PM
  • Outreach Events
