About Our Church

Learn about our history, beliefs, and mission

Our Founder: Apostle Isaac Mbale

Apostle Isaac Mbale founded Salvation Church for All Nations with a vision to create a church that transcends cultural and national boundaries. His leadership and dedication have guided SCAN since its establishment.

Our History

Salvation Church for All Nations was established in Karonga with a divine mandate to spread the gospel to all nations. From humble beginnings, the church has grown into a vibrant community impacting lives across the region.

1995

SCAN founded by Apostle Isaac Mbale

2000

Church headquarters established in Karonga

2010

Expansion to multiple communities
